Privacy Policy

Your trust is our top priority. Learn how we protect your data and privacy.

Introduction

At Ruchii Tiffin, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information. Your trust is at the heart of everything we do, and we strive to ensure the highest level of transparency and security. By accessing our website, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect both personal and non-personal information. Personal information includes your name, email, phone number, delivery address, and payment details. This information is essential for processing your orders, communicating with you, and providing a seamless experience. Non-personal information includes your IP address, browser type, and browsing activity, which helps us understand website usage and improve functionality.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to fulfill your orders, manage deliveries, enhance your experience on our website, and send updates, offers, and newsletters with your consent. Additionally, your data allows us to personalize your interactions and ensure a smooth, secure, and efficient service.

Sharing Your Information

Your data may be shared with trusted third parties, including delivery partners for fulfilling orders, payment processors for secure transactions, and analytics services to improve our website functionality. We ensure that any third party involved in handling your data adheres to the highest standards of data protection.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Cookies are used to enhance your browsing experience by remembering your preferences and improving website performance. These small files help tailor our services to your needs. You can manage your cookie preferences directly through your browser settings, ensuring you maintain control over your privacy.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. You can also opt out of marketing communications and request details about how your data is used. To exercise your rights, contact us at support@ruchiitiffin.com. We are committed to ensuring that your data is handled responsibly and transparently.
